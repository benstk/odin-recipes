Ribeye Steak

Description

A creamy pasta for people who love the milky texture and salty text of bacon. We are, however, not using cream, as that would be a cardinal sin.

All you need is to cook the pasta first. While it is cooking, beat one whole egg and mix it with cheddar cheese until it becomes a wet sticky texture. Heat up the pan and add in the bacon, really letting the oil seep out of the bacon. Leave the oil in the pan and place the bacon on a plate. Pour the cooked pasta into the oiled pan and add in the egg and cheese mixture. Now watch the pasta and flip it, occasionally adding pasta water if it gets too dry. You want to hear the sound of wet sex, and that is when you know the Carbonara is perfect. Now sprinkle the bacon and generously sprinkle cheddar cheese on top too.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Egg
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Boil water in pot
  2. Place pasta in boiling water
  3. Pan fry bacon
  4. Mix cheese and egg in a bowl
  5. Remove bacon and add pasta to pan
  6. Add bowl mixture
  7. Flip and add pasta water as needed
  8. Sprinkle bacon and cheese on top
  9. Serve
